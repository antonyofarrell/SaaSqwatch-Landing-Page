Frequently asked

Questions we get in the comments.

Do vendors pay for reviews? +

No. We use affiliate links on tools we already recommend — they pay us a referral if you sign up, but the verdict is locked before the link is. Vendors can't buy tier placement and we don't take sponsored reviews.

How long is a "real" test? +

Forty hours of hands-on, minimum, on a live project with a deadline. Side-by-side comparisons go longer — episode 042 took two of us four weeks.

Can I suggest a tool? +

Yes — reply to any Tuesday email with a name and we add it to the queue. Subscribers-only; it keeps the signal high.

What about tools I already pay for? +

The tier list moves. If a tool you use falls from A to C, you'll see it in the Tuesday dispatch with the reason and a migration note.

Do you cover AI tools? +

About half the queue now. Same rules: a real project, a real deadline. If it can't hold up for forty hours, it can't be S-tier.

Where's the channel? +

YouTube primary, with full transcripts and scorecards on the site. RSS for the written reviews if that's how you live.
